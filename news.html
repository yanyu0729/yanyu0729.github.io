March 2026

New Article Published in Nature Food

Nature Food

I am excited to share that our paper, A framework for estimating manure nitrogen balance and recycling potential for current and future conditions in the USA, was published in Nature Food.

The study presents a national framework for quantifying manure nitrogen balance and recycling potential across the United States, providing new insights into improving nutrient circularity and sustainable agricultural systems.

This work was conducted in collaboration with colleagues from the USDA Agricultural Research Service and the University of Maryland Center for Environmental Science.
