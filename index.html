About

I am an environmental scientist working at the intersection of sustainable agriculture, nitrogen management, nutrient circularity, and food systems. My work aims to generate scientific evidence that supports more sustainable and resilient agri-food systems.

Research

My research is organized around three connected themes.

Sustainable Agriculture & Food Systems

How can agriculture feed a growing population while reducing environmental impacts?

Nutrient Circularity

How can nutrient circularity improve agricultural sustainability?

Science for Decision-Making

How can science support evidence-based agricultural policy?
