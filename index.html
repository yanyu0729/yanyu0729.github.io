I am an environmental scientist developing a systems-level understanding of nitrogen cycling to inform sustainable nitrogen management.

My research explores how nitrogen can be used more efficiently and circularly across agricultural, food, biofuel, and waste systems to maximize its benefits for food production while minimizing environmental impacts in the U.S. and around the world.
