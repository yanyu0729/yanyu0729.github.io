Building science-based solutions for sustainable agriculture and resilient food systems.

I study sustainable agriculture and food systems through systems modeling, geospatial analysis, nutrient budgeting, and stakeholder engagement.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Georgetown University Earth Commons. My research focuses on sustainable nutrient management, agricultural systems, nitrogen circularity, and the food–energy–water nexus.
